The Artist

Ana Silva

Founder & Creative Director

I grew up in Caracas, where nature was never something you visited; it was simply where you lived. Macaws on your window, green in every direction, and a closeness to the earth that shaped everything I see and everything I make.

I’ve always believed that beauty isn’t something you create; it’s something you uncover. A texture that was already waiting. A color that was already there. A form that nature had already begun.

MOSU is simply my way of listening to that. Each composition is a conversation between my hands and the materials.

“Every chapter of my life has taught me the same thing; beauty is always there, even when everything else changes. You just have to be still enough to see it, and brave enough to build something from it.”

The Making

Behind Every Piece

01

It Begins with a Feeling

Before any material is chosen, there is a space, and the emotion it wants to hold. Light, architecture, silence. Every composition starts by listening to what the room is asking for.

02

Chosen by Hand, Guided by Instinct

Each variety of moss carries its own texture, tone, and character. Ana selects every element individually, drawn to what the piece needs, not what convention expects.

03

No Two Pieces Are the Same

Nature never repeats itself, and neither do we. Every finished work carries its own character, a living expression of the moment it was made.

The Nature of Moss

It asks for nothing. No water. No sunlight. No soil. Preserved moss keeps its texture and color for years; quietly, gracefully, without a single demand.

And still, it shifts something in the room. Nature has always had this effect on us; a gentle ease, a deeper breath, a space that finally feels at rest.
